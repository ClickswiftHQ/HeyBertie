<h2>Finding a Groomer</h2>
<p>Browse groomer profiles on heyBertie to find the right fit for your pet. Each profile shows their services, prices, availability, and location so you can compare options easily.</p>

<h2>Choosing a Service</h2>
<p>Once you've found a groomer, select the service you need from their profile page. You'll see the price and estimated duration for each option.</p>

<h2>Selecting a Date and Time</h2>
<p>The booking calendar shows available time slots based on the groomer's schedule. Pick a date and time that works for you. Only available slots are shown, so you won't need to worry about double-bookings.</p>

<h2>Entering Your Details</h2>
<p>Provide your contact details and any notes about your pet (breed, temperament, special requirements). If you already have a heyBertie account, some of this will be pre-filled for you.</p>

<h3>Booking as a Guest</h3>
<p>You don't need an account to book — just enter your name and email address. You'll receive a confirmation email with a link to manage your booking.</p>

<h3>Booking with an Account</h3>
<p>If you're logged in, your details are saved and you can manage all your bookings from your account dashboard.</p>

<h2>Confirmation</h2>
<p>After completing your booking, you'll see a confirmation page with all the details. A confirmation email is also sent to you with everything you need to know, including the groomer's address and any preparation tips.</p>

<h2>Managing Your Booking</h2>
<p>Need to reschedule or cancel? Use the link in your confirmation email or log in to your account to manage your booking. Check your groomer's cancellation policy for any time limits or fees.</p>
