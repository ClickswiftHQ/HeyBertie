<h2>Accessing Your Services</h2>
<p>From your dashboard, navigate to the Services section. Here you can view all your current services and add new ones.</p>

<h2>Creating a New Service</h2>
<p>Click "Add Service" and fill in the details:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Name</strong> — Choose a clear, descriptive name (e.g., "Full Groom — Small Dog")</li>
<li><strong>Description</strong> — Explain what's included so pet owners know what to expect</li>
<li><strong>Duration</strong> — How long the appointment takes in minutes</li>
<li><strong>Price</strong> — The cost of the service</li>
</ul>

<h2>Location-Specific Services</h2>
<p>If you operate from multiple locations, you can assign services to specific locations or make them available across all locations. This is useful if you offer different services at different sites.</p>

<h2>Editing and Removing Services</h2>
<p>You can edit any service at any time from the Services page. Changes will apply to future bookings only — existing bookings keep the original service details.</p>
<p>To remove a service, click the delete option. Note that services with upcoming bookings cannot be deleted until those bookings are completed or cancelled.</p>

<h2>Tips for Great Service Listings</h2>
<ul>
<li>Use clear, specific names — "Bath & Tidy" is better than "Service A"</li>
<li>Include what's covered in the description (wash, dry, nail trim, etc.)</li>
<li>Set realistic durations to avoid overbooking</li>
<li>Keep your prices up to date</li>
</ul>
