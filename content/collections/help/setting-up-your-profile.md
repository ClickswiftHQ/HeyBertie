<h2>Creating Your Account</h2>
<p>Getting started on heyBertie takes just a few minutes. Visit the <a href="/register">sign up page</a> and create your account with your email address. You'll be guided through the onboarding process step by step.</p>

<h2>Business Details</h2>
<p>During onboarding, you'll be asked to provide your core business information:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Business name</strong> — The name pet owners will see when browsing</li>
<li><strong>Handle</strong> — Your unique URL (e.g., heybertie.com/your-handle)</li>
<li><strong>Description</strong> — A short summary of what you offer</li>
<li><strong>Location</strong> — Your salon address or service area</li>
</ul>

<h2>Adding Your Services</h2>
<p>Once your profile is set up, head to your dashboard to add the services you offer. For each service you can set:</p>
<ul>
<li>Service name and description</li>
<li>Duration and price</li>
<li>Whether the service is available at specific locations</li>
</ul>
<p>See our <a href="/help/adding-services">Adding Services</a> article for a detailed walkthrough.</p>

<h2>Setting Your Availability</h2>
<p>Configure your working hours from the dashboard so pet owners can only book when you're available. You can set recurring weekly hours and block out specific dates for holidays.</p>

<h2>Going Live</h2>
<p>Once you've added at least one service and set your availability, your profile will be visible to pet owners searching on heyBertie. You'll start receiving booking requests straight away.</p>
